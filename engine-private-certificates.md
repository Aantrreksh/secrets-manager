# Preparing to generate private certificates
{: #prepare-generate-certificates}

You can enable your {{site.data.keyword.secrets-manager_full}} service instance to generate private certificates by configuring the private certificates engine.
{: shortdesc}

In {{site.data.keyword.secrets-manager_short}}, the private certificates engine serves as the back end for the `private_cert` secret type. Private certificates are domain-validated TLS certificates that you can generate and manage in the service. Before you can create a private certificate, you must enable your service instance by creating a root certificate authority and a valid chain of trust for your certificates.
